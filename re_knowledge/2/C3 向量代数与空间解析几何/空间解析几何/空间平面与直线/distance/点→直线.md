### 7. 点到直线的距离

<ul>

点 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 到直线 $\frac{x-x_{1}}{l}=\frac{y-y_{1}}{m}=\frac{z-z_{1}}{n}$ 的距离为

$$
d=\frac{\left|\left(x_{1}-x_{0}, y_{1}-y_{0}, z_{1}-z_{0}\right) \times (l, m, n)\right|}{\sqrt{l^{2}+m^{2}+n^{2}}}
$$
